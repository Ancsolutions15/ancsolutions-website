Our Story

Built on
Trust &
Reliability

ANC Solutions was founded with one goal: to become the most dependable electrical and electronics supply partner for large-scale Indian industries. We understand that production downtime is not an option — so we make sure it never becomes one.

We work closely with procurement teams, project managers, and maintenance engineers to ensure the right components reach the right place at the right time. Our network of certified suppliers ensures quality is never compromised.

From single-unit sourcing to long-term bulk supply contracts, we scale our service to your operational needs.

What We Supply

Our Product
Range

We supply a comprehensive range of electrical and electronics components for industrial applications. All products are available in bulk quantities with customised procurement support.

Electrical
Cables & Wiring

Industrial-grade power and control cables for high-voltage and low-voltage applications across plant environments.

  • Armoured & unarmoured cables
  • Fire-resistant variants available
  • Custom lengths on order
  • ISI marked products
Electrical
Switchgear & MCBs

Circuit breakers, isolators, contactors, and distribution boards from leading industrial brands.

  • MCBs, MCCBs, ACBs
  • Contactors & overload relays
  • Distribution boards
  • HV & LV switchgear
Electrical
Transformers

Distribution transformers, isolation transformers, and auto-transformers for industrial power management.

  • 11KV / 33KV distribution
  • Dry-type & oil-cooled
  • Custom ratings available
  • BIS certified
Electronics
PLCs & Controllers

Programmable logic controllers, HMIs, and industrial automation controllers for process control.

  • Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Delta
  • HMI panels & displays
  • I/O modules & accessories
  • Programming support available
Electronics
Sensors & Instruments

Proximity sensors, pressure transmitters, temperature sensors, and industrial measurement instruments.

  • Proximity & photoelectric sensors
  • Pressure & flow transmitters
  • Thermocouples & RTDs
  • Level indicators
Electrical
Motors & Drives

AC/DC motors, variable frequency drives, and servo drives for industrial machinery and conveyor systems.

  • IE2 & IE3 efficiency motors
  • VFDs — 0.5HP to 500HP
  • Servo motors & drives
  • Gearboxes & couplings
